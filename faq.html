Frequently Asked Questions

How can I enroll on a course?

It's a very simple process! You can click on "Get Started" on the course page, or go to the Sim2Reality Udemy profile and choose from the list of published courses there. Creating a Udemy account is free, and the platform is used world-wide.

Can I do a trial for a course?

No, but while not all of our courses are paid, they are all created to the same high standards. Try out one of our free courses to get an idea what the paid content is like.

Can I contact the instructor to ask for help?

Absolutely! In fact, it's encouraged. Udemy has a fantastic Q&A area in each course, to allow you to ask the instructor questions about the content. There, you may ask anything you're unsure about.

Will there be more content?

Yes there will! The aim of Sim2Reality is to bring to simulator pilots an authentic pilot training experience, "from zero to hero," and beyond! With that goal in mind, new coureses are constantly being developed.
